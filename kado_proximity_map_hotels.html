Proximity Map of Hotels Around DEER Nigeria (Kado)

This map shows the driving time from various hotels to DEER Nigeria (Kado) to support efficient cooking gas delivery to these areas.

Use the legend in the top-right corner to switch between satellite and standard views, and to turn map layers on or off as needed.

The red marker at the center represents the location of DEER Nigeria (Kado). Tap on each marker to view the name of the corresponding hotel.
