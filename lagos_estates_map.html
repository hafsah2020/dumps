Map of Residential Estates between Lekki phase 1 toll gate and DEER Nigeria (Ibeju)

This map provides information on the driving time from various estates to the identified host areas.

The estates considered are located along the Lekkiâ€“Epe corridor, between Ibeju and the Lekki Phase 1 Toll Gate.

Potential host areas were identified based on the names of major settlements along the corridor. The centre point of each area was obtained from Google Maps, and a 20-minute drive-time buffer was generated around each centre. Estates falling within these buffers were then allocated to their respective host areas.

Use the legend in the top-right corner of the map to switch between satellite and standard views, and to turn map layers on or off as needed. Tap on each marker to view the name of the corresponding estate.
